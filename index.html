Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Evelyn Stone, DDS

Dr. Evelyn Stone completed her dental training at Columbia University School of Dental Medicine and has spent over 12 years perfecting gentle dentistry techniques. Her mission is to dispel clinical anxiety and make dental visits comfortable, educational, and relaxing.

Anxiety-Reducing Technology

Advanced Technology Designed For Comfort

We leverage modern technology not only for diagnostic precision but to ensure a quick, painless experience.

Digital X-Rays

No uncomfortable plates inside your mouth. Offers immediate visualization and outputs 90% less radiation than legacy systems.

Painless Anesthesia

Our computer-guided local anesthetic delivery is nearly imperceptible, removing the anxiety and dread of traditional syringes.

Intraoral Cameras

High-definition tiny cameras let you see exactly what Dr. Stone sees on our chairside screens, supporting absolute dental transparency.

3D Diagnostics

Cone Beam CT scanning constructs complete 3D digital models of jaw tissues, maximizing implant success and root structure analysis.
